Who is the SWAPO Party Youth League?

The SPYL is a progressive youth political wing, born out of the crucibles of the anticolonial struggle for freedom and national independence, and embodies the same principles as those of SWAPO Party.

SPYL serves as an active, revolutionary, and militant transmitting belt of the SWAPO Party ideology, policies and programmes.
